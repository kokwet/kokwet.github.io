About Us

We are a grassroots community based organization (CBO), consisting of local residents, friends and partners of a rural community in remote Kenya: Kapkoros location in Narok County. Our mission is to uplift the living standards of people of the community by promoting education, health and community development in the community.

Our community consists of local residents, friends and partners committed to uplift the living standards of people of Kapkoros area. Kapkoros area lies under the administrative unit of Kapkoros sublocation in Trans Mara East Sub-County in Narok County, Kenya. The area is about 60km from the famous Maasai Mara game reserve.

Caroline Baraza

Twitter Facebook Google+

"I have seen Kokwet CBO help improve the performance of students in our local primary schools. In 2014, Kokwet CBO was able to bring together students from six local schools for academic competitions and motivational talks.These students performed better in the national examinations compared to the students in the previous years. About 10 of the students joined the best schools in the country. We welcome you to join us in promoting healthy competitions in our primary schools."

Caroline is a patient attendant at a private clinic.

Philip Rotich

Twitter Facebook Google+

“Our community - Kapkoros sub-location, is one of the poorest in our county and the schools within the sub-location have been performing poorly. This is why we started Kokwet CBO. Holding field days in schools has been one of our successful programs. In 2014, we had five field in schools. We held motivational talks and covered health education namely sanitation and hygiene, nutrition, de-worming of students, H.I.V/Aids awareness and discouraged FGM, early pregnancies and early marriages. Through such programs, we will continue to work to eradicate poverty in our community.”

Philip is a clinical officer in the Masai Mara.
